Quickstart

Formula Wars in one minute

Movement never stops. Build formulas while dodging, then decide whether to attack now or run for a stronger line.

Player 1 uses WASD. Player 2 uses Arrow keys. In CPU mode, the orange player acts automatically.

Use the turn console to verify formulas, equip known ones, attack, run, steal, or duplicate physics variables.

Physics variables are one-time arena resources. Duplicate them to reserve copies, or consume them directly for mobility effects.

Counters matter. Limits answer infinity, diameter breaks pi traps, integrals reverse derivatives, and conjugates answer complex attacks.
